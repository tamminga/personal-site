Welcome

I am an Associate Professor of Linguistics at the University of Pennsylvania, where I direct the Language Variation and Cognition Lab. I’m also affiliated with the Social and Behavioral Sciences Initiative, the Social and Cultural Evolution Working Group, and Integrated Language Sciences and Technology.
